Little about me

Weyatta Rosemarie Mckeever, alternatively known as Sassy MC is a young, intelligent, outstanding, innovative, and well-spoken Radio and TV personality. Ms. Mckeever has worked in the capacity of a presenter, talk show host, station manager, and administrative assistant, brand influencer and presently a Director of Radio, amongst other things, at several top-notched media institutions in Liberia including Kool 91.9, Hott FM, Boss TV and Voice of Liberia radio station. Miss Mckeever started her career as a child broadcaster in Grade 7 and has since made a name for herself as one of Liberia’s brightest radio personalities. Impressively, she is a graduate Of AME University, where she earned a BA degree with honor in Mass Communications and Public Administration. As a young Liberian woman, Ms. Mckeever’s dream is not only limited to being a professional international Talk show host, but to also become an author, an air hostess, and a fashion designer. Sassy has covered a lot of social and cooperate programs serving as Master of ceremony including Miss Sinkor Beauty Pageant 2019/2020, Opening of a French Restaurant, Ms. Liberia 2022 Red Carpet Host, Miss Earth 2022 launch red carpet, Radical women movement of Liberia’s Empowerment Conference, etc., and several others. She is also a Brand Influencer at DKT Liberia. Miss Mckeever is a Voice actor and has recorded several adverts for many top-notched companies including Tipme , and her most recent, With Jeety Trading company. Miss Mckeever believes, that with persistency, determination, and support, she can and will achieve all of her aforementioned goals.
